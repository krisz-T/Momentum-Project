# Momentum - Feature Decomposition

**Version:** 1.0.0

This document breaks down the core features of the Momentum application into distinct user stories, defining the functional scope of the project.

---

### US-01: Workout Logging
**As an** Athlete,
**I want to** log my completed workouts with details like type and duration,
**So that** I can keep a record of my activity.

### US-02: Gamification Engine
**As an** Athlete,
**I want to** earn Experience Points (XP) and unlock badges for my activity,
**So that** I feel motivated to stay consistent.

### US-03: Global Leaderboards
**As an** Athlete,
**I want to** view a global ranking of users based on their total XP,
**So that** I can see how I stack up against the competition.

### US-04: Structured Training
**As an** Athlete,
**I want to** browse and enroll in structured training plans,
**So that** I have a guided path for my fitness journey.

### US-05: Dynamic Content Management
**As an** Admin,
**I want to** create, update, and delete training plans and exercises,
**So that** I can keep the application's content fresh and engaging.

### US-06: User Moderation
**As an** Admin,
**I want to** ban and unban users,
**So that** I can maintain a fair and positive community environment.
